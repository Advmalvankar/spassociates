About SP Associates

Founded in 2008 by Adv. Siddhesh Vitthal Patil, SP Associates is a full-service law firm headquartered in Vashi, Navi Mumbai. With a legacy of over 17 years of undefeated practice, the firm has earned distinction for its ethical, client-centric, and result-oriented approach to legal representation across multiple domains of law.

Guided by the standards laid down under the Advocates Act, 1961, SP Associates upholds the highest ideals of the legal profession—integrity, independence, diligence, and accountability. The firm's ethos embodies the duties of an advocate as an officer of the court, ensuring justice is pursued not merely as a profession, but as a solemn responsibility to society.

Under the leadership of Adv. Siddhesh Patil, who also serves as the General Secretary of Adhivakta Parishad, Navi Mumbai, the firm has built a reputation for professionalism, precision, and honesty in every facet of legal practice. Each member of the firm is committed to maintaining impartial conduct, respecting professional confidentiality, and providing competent legal services in accordance with statutory norms and professional ethics.

SP Associates operates on the principle that justice is paramount. The firm's practice spans civil, criminal, corporate, constitutional, and property law, among other areas, delivering comprehensive legal solutions to individuals, businesses, and institutions. Through a balanced blend of advocacy, advisory, and negotiation, SP Associates continues to contribute to the growth of an equitable and transparent legal system in India.

With a dedicated team of associates and trainees, the firm nurtures a culture of continuous learning and professional excellence. Its commitment to public service, ethical advocacy, and respect for the rule of law stands as a testament to its longstanding values and its vision for a just and responsible legal community.

Practice Areas

Civil Litigation: Property, recovery, injunctions, tenancy, and contract cases; strategic advocacy before courts.
Criminal Law: Bail, trials, appeals, and revisions; adherence to CrPC and IPC procedures.
Family and Divorce Law: Matrimonial disputes, divorce, maintenance, custody, and domestic violence; focus on mediation.
Banking & NI Act (138): Cheque dishonour, banking disputes, loan recoveries, and DRT matters.
Corporate and Contract Law: Mergers, incorporations, contract drafting, compliance, and dispute resolution.
Consultation & Legal Opinions: Well-reasoned legal opinions and strategic advice.
Society, Trust & Notary Matters: Registrations, trust formation, compliance, and certified notarial services.
Property and Real Estate Law: Conveyancing, title verification, joint development, redevelopment, RERA compliance.
Arbitration & ADR: Arbitration, mediation, conciliation; confidentiality and swift settlements.
Consumer Protection Law: Representation before Consumer Commissions for deficiency and unfair practices.
Labour and Employment Law: Labour compliance, wrongful termination, industrial dispute; representation before tribunals.
Constitutional & Writ Jurisdiction: Writ petitions in High Courts and Supreme Court.
Notary Services: Attestation, verification, certification, notarization as per Notaries Act, 1952.
